KI TIERS
Don't you love match-up charts? The problem is no single person has the complete knowledge to generate an accurate chart for an entire game. This chart is an effort to bridge that gap of kknowledge.

How this works:
  • Each character's data is backed with a MU chart provided by a character specialist
  • Tiers are calculated using 2x the standard deviation as upper and lower bounds (S and F tier)
  • Overall character score is based on sum of winning, losing, and even matchups
  • The overlayed number if the character's position on the overall tier list
  • Hover over icons to show specific matchup numbers
  • Confidence level is the percentage of other contributers whose data agrees
